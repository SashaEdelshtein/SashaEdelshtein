Teplo Project

Teplo is a Moscow-based company seeking to solve problem with cold hands and feet during winter time in Russia, which key products are hand and feet warmers.

The goal was to create a package which would be sold on ice rinks and parks as well as in hotels. Therefore, target audience is people, who spend their time outside during winter period.

As most competitors are focused on sport theme, we decided to create a package that would both tell people about the product and graphically rhyme with entironment.
